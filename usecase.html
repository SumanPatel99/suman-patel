Use Cases

Use Cases

Era Swap Life Wallet

Era Swap Life is a Single Sign-On (SSO) gateway that provides access to multiple utility platforms accepting Era Swap (ES) Utility token. Those utility can be stored in Era Swap Life wallet securely on Blockchain.

Swappers Wall

Swappers Wall is a secured social sharing and rewarding platform that promotes and facilitates interaction between friends, family, peers, and colleagues. It is a place where you can connect with your friends securely.

TimeAlly

TimeAlly is a Decentralized App (DApp) based on a pre-defined set of rules through Smart Contracts that offers additional ES counts on staking plans to the users.

Time Swappers

Time Swappers is a blockchain-based transparent freelancing platform. It is a peer to peer marketplace that enables buyers and sellers to exchange services in the form of ES, which means no interference of any third parties and no multilayer charges.

Era Swap Academy

Era Swap Academy is an E-Mart for Subject Matter Expert (SME) to list their diversified skill & expertise for the new learners and enthusiasts. It bridges the gap for any new learner to learn directly from SME..

Day Swappers

Day Swappers is a unique affiliate program for Era Swap Community members to earn rewards for exchanging services, influencing and adding new users. Anybody who believes in their marketing or influencing skills can become a Day Swapper.

BetDeEx

BetDeEx is a decentralized prediction platform where users can predict and win. It is a decentralized, transparent, and trustless platform. On BetDeEx you can predict on event such as Sports, Politics, Movies, Tech, Science, Entertainment, and many more.

BuzCafe

BuzCafe is a network of outlets for Era Swap Community who meets up locally to exchange services, and where users can buy services in a peer to peer mode by meeting up.

Upcoming Projects

ComputeEx

ComputeEx comes with a multi-exchange solution for Era Swap users by fetching deals from various Digital Asset exchanges across the world and Peer to Peer Lend & Borrow in a transparent manner to the users.

Value of Farmers

An app for farmer`s to make their life better, where they can view their land, choose & sell crops, swap farming tools and machines. Basically, our goal is to make a platform where everything is on farmer’s fingertips.

Date Swappers

Date Swappers is a next-gen dating platform, where strangers can meet and hook up, Date Swapper is not only for teens and singles but also for Business owners and professionals where they can meet various business people and appoint a business meeting.
